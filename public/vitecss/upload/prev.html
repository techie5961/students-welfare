Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about earning, payments, and community support.

How do I start earning on Earnify?+
Simply sign up, choose your region (Nigeria or Cameroon), pay the activation fee (₦14,000 or 5,800 XAF) to unlock instant bonus, and start playing Killer Bean or invite friends to earn agent rewards. You'll also get access to weekly support allowance.
Is Earnify certified and legally registered?+
Yes, Earnify Ventures Ltd is fully incorporated under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Our operations comply with all local financial and data regulations, ensuring security and trust.
How often is the weekly support allowance paid?+
Weekly support distribution runs every Wednesday and Sunday via automated system. Eligible members receive between ₦20k–₦40k (Nigeria) or up to 16,500 XAF (Cameroon) directly to their registered account.
What is the Killer Bean game reward?+
Killer Bean is an interactive board-style game on Earnify. Every player who participates has guaranteed win amounts between ₦10,000 – ₦20,000 based on performance. It's designed for fun and real monetary returns.
Can I really receive a free loan from the pool?+
Yes! The Earnify Free Loan Pool provides qualified members access to loans up to ₦500,000 (or 200,000 XAF) with zero interest and flexible repayment terms. Conditions apply based on activity level and loyalty.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We support bank transfers, mobile money (for Cameroon), and secure in-app wallets. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours on business days, and you can track your transaction history directly on your dashboard.
